## Safari

### Enable tab key navigation

- This is required for correctly debugging and auditing - don’t forget to implement this!

#### Steps

1. Open Safari browser, then go to the menu bar and choose _Safari Preferences_
2. Click the **Advanced** tab
3. Mark the accessibility option that lets you **Press Tab to highlight each item on a webpage**
